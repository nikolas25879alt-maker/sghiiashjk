How It Works

Running in under
fifteen minutes

01
Plug in the EkoSlnko Bridge

Our compact device connects to your inverter's RS485 or local WiFi port. No rewiring. No electrician. Just plug it in and it pairs automatically.

02

Scan the QR code on the device, enter your panel count and energy tariff. The dashboard goes live in under two minutes.

03
